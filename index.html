About Me

Hello, my name is Hunter Cline. I grew up in a very small town in rural Indiana called Union City. I graduated from Union City Jr/Sr High School in 2016 with fifty-four other students, just to give you an idea of how small of a community it was. I began my college career at Indiana University East, located in Richmond, IN, in August 2016. I majored in Informatics and graduated in December 2019 with my Bachelor's degree.
I have worked as a Helpdesk Technician for several months since graduating, but my passion lies with programming. I am currently enrolled in Altcademy which is a paid, online, certificate program for full-stack web development to increase my chances of becoming a junior developer. As of now, I am actively searching for an entry level software development/web development position in the Indianapolis area.

Although most of my time is taken up by work, coursework, or personal projects, I still manage to have more hobbies than I need. During the summer, I spend a lot of my free time golfing. This is something that my friends and I can do for an entire day and have a blast the whole time. I also love to watch all kinds of professional sports too, like football, basketball, and especially MMA(UFC). My other main hobbies are PC gaming and exercising.
